Epic Games is a game and software development company based in North Caroline. Epic Games develops their engine knows as the Unreal Engine in order to internally run and develop their vide games, such as Fortnite. The Unreal Engine was named the most successful video game engine by Guinness. Epic Games even has their own game store where you can buy all of their games.

Epic Games' most popular game is Fortnite (2017), Unreal, Infinity Blade, Shadow Complex, Battle Breakers, Gears of War, and more. Fortnite, being their most popular game, had gained over 125 Million players in the first year of its release.
