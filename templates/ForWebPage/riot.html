Riot Games

Company Logo About Riot Games Popular Games By Riot

Riot Games was originally created by Brandon Beck and Mark Merrill, when they were friends in college and shared a love for video games. As the company grew, they began to test their first game, League of Legends, which is their most popular game to date. Valorant, one of their newer games, was released in 2020, and has many esports teams surrounding it.

Riot Games has developed many popular games, including Valorant, League of Legends, Teamfight Tactics, Legends of Runeterra, and Rising Thunder.
